# Technical Overview: The Settlement Pipeline

Spacedeck Protocol is the high-velocity clearinghouse for **Autonomous Transaction Settlement**. We are positioned at Layer 4 of the new **AI DeFi Supply Chain**, serving as the narrowest bottleneck for institutional capital flow.

## The 6-Layer Ecosystem (Supply Chain Alignment)

1.  **Layer 1 (The Compute):** GPU Clusters & Foundation Models (Intelligence Origin).
2.  **Layer 2 (The Frameworks):** Eliza, Zerebro, Rig (The Agentic Operating Systems).
3.  **Layer 3 (The Agents):** Developer-built bots managing treasuries and retail intents (The Customers).
4.  **Layer 4 (The Spacedeck Prism):** Our Protocol. Intent Translation & Security Gating.
5.  **Layer 5 (The Solvers):** Institutional Market Makers (Liquidity Fulfillment).
6.  **Layer 6 (The Blockchain):** Solana / Sepolia (Final Physical Settlement).

---

## The Execution Lifecycle

### 1. Inbound Gateway (The Prism)
The Gateway parses raw AI prose into a structured **GoldenPayload**. It uses LLM-optimized logic to extract parameters and calculates the optimal **Strike Vector** for frictionless execution.

### 2. Risk Sentinel (The Compliance Guardian)
The Sentinel physically gates every intent. It performs real-time checks against global sanctions lists (OFAC) to ensure only compliant liquidity enters the **Settlement Fabric**.

### 3. Marketplace Auction
Spacedeck broadcasts authorized intents to our **Institutional Solver Network**. The auction seleccion criteria focus on maximum net user surplus and execution velocity.

### 4. Kinetic Strike (Physical Settlement)
The Strike engine orchestrates finality. It leverages **MPC-signing** and solver-validated liquidity to close the transaction on the target blockchain.

## Infrastructure Reliability
Spacedeck is designed for **Atomic Reliability**. The protocol decouples **Capital Authorization** (EIP-2612) from **Intent Authorization** (EIP-712), allowing agents to command institutional mass without ever exposing private keys.
